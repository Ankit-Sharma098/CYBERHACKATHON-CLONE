About

The Cyber Hackathon is organized by the Economic Offences Unit (EoU) Bihar Police, in collaboration with C-DAC Patna, a scientific society under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. The Cyber Hackathon 2025 offers an exciting platform for participants to showcase their expertise in cybersecurity, digital forensics, and innovative software solutions. This event aims to inspire creativity and innovation in tackling challenges related to cybersecurity and forensics. It encourages participants to apply their skills and knowledge to develop impactful solutions that contribute to a sustainable future.
